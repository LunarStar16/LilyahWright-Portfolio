Biography

Lilyah Wright

Lilyah is an avid gamer. She has played video games since she was young and has always wanted to get into a creative career so that she can show off her creativity and give other people enjoyment through interactive storytelling and escapism like video games can do.

To get herself further into the video game development career, Lilyah is working on getting her Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in New Media at the University of Lethbridge. Lilyah is acquiring many different skills such as, leadership, various communication skills, design, art, animation, coding, and many other skills that improves her understanding of the game development process.

Lilyah’s repertoire of skills and knowledge continues to grow and she looks forward to the future where she will gain more experience in her desired field of game development and storytelling.

Works

I like to create art in all different kinds of forms, whether that be 2d, 3d or even with words. Creativity and storytelling through as many ways possible is what I like to do, if that be concept art, writing, 3d modelling, animation, or even through coding. Which is why I love video games since so many aspects of what I like to do goes into making them. My favorite video game genre is fantasy and my biggest inspiration for my art is The Elder Scrolls: Skyrim, where its fantasy world allowed me to escape from my own into a land filled with magic and dragons, where I can be who I want within its fantasy setting. Fantasy allowed me to escape from my own world and let me be who I want to be, which is why I love it so much, whether it’s the more stereotypical medieval style, or a more futuristic sci-fi, all types of fantasy are what inspire my art and creativity.
